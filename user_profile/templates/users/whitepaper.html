What is XEAM?

XEAM is a digital ERC-20 token designed to create transparent income streams supporting NGOs and food banks. A portion of each transaction is automatically routed to a community-controlled charity wallet.

Transaction Fee Structure

Buy (10%)
  • 3% to Liquidity
  • 4% to Charity Fund
  • 1% to Growth
  • 1% to Operations
  • 1% to Emergency
Sell (12%)
  • 4% to Liquidity
  • 4% to Charity Fund
  • 2% to Growth
  • 1% to Operations
  • 1% to Emergency

All fees are redistributed on-chain and contribute to project sustainability.

Token Utility

  • Staking: Lock XEAM to earn USDC rewards.
  • Charity Support: 4% of each tx goes to vetted nonprofits via The Giving Block.
  • Emergency Fund: Decentralized safety reserve managed via DAO.
  • Inspire: Smart liquidity enhancer algorithm.
